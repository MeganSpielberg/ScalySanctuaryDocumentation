# Surveys

These are the knowledge questionnaires used in the pre-test → treatment
→ post-test procedure described in [Study Design](Study-Design_458765.md)
and [Data Collection Setup](Data-Collection-Setup_393217.md). The
pre-quiz establishes each participant's baseline reptile care knowledge
before they are assigned to a condition; the post-quiz uses equivalent
questions to measure knowledge gain afterward. The difference between
the two scores is the knowledge gain dependent variable used in
[Data Analysis](Data-Analysis_950273.md).
