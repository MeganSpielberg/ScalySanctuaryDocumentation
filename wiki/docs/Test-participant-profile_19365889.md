# Test participant profile

This profile defines the recruitment criteria used during participant
screening, referenced in [Night of the Nerds](Night-of-the-Nerds_19202049.md)
as "the study's eligibility criteria."

**Hard Requirements (Must Have):** little/no reptile caretaking
experience, proficient in English, proficient with modern technology,
physically able to play the game, able to read.

**Preferred Characteristics (Nice to Have):**

- *Interest & Motivation:* interest in reptiles, affinity with games,
  interest in casual games.
- *Demographics:* age 13-35, even gender split, diverse ethnicity
  sample.

> ℹ️ **Note:** The age range above (13-35) reflects the original
> profile. Following a discussion of the ethical considerations
> involved in testing with minors, the decision was made to move away
> from recruiting participants below age 16.
