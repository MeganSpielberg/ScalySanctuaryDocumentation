## Information Brochure and Informed Consent Form: Teaching About Reptile Caretaking

### 💡 Introduction & Purpose

Thank you for participating. The information brochure before you
explains the details of this study. If you have any questions at any
point, please feel free to ask one of the researchers.

This project focuses on improving reptile care education and is
conducted by Fontys Master students Megan Spielberg and Valentino Clerx.
The aim is to examine how specific learning elements affect engagement
and knowledge retention.

### 📋 What to Expect

In this study, you will go through a few different stages which will
take approximately 25 minutes in total.

1.  You will start with an initial questionnaire to gauge your current
    knowledge on the topic.

2.  Following this, we will present you with some information regarding
    the proper care and keeping of reptiles.

3.  To conclude the session, you will complete a final questionnaire
    regarding the information presented and your overall experience.

### 🔒 Data Collection & Confidentiality

The data that is collected will be treated confidentially at all times.
Your identity will not be linked to your responses in any published
reports, and all data will be securely stored for future research
purposes.

### ⚠️ Risks and Benefits

There are no known risks associated with participating. While there are
no direct personal benefits, your participation will contribute to
research on learning through digital experiences.

### ✋ Participant Rights

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You are free to withdraw at
any time, for any reason, without any negative consequences.
